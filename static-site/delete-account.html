Request Account Deletion

To delete your GoodWatch Movies account and all associated data, please send an email request to our support team. We will process your request and permanently delete your account.

Request Account Deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account information (email, profile data)
  • Streaming platform preferences
  • Watch history and watchlist
  • Personalization data and preferences
  • Language and mood preferences
  • All recommendation history

Processing Time: Your deletion request will be processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
