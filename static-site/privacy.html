Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

GoodWatch collects minimal data to provide movie recommendations:

  • Preferences: Your selected streaming platforms, languages, and mood preferences
  • Watchlist: Movies you save for later
  • Interaction data: Which recommendations you accept or skip (to improve future picks)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone) — unless you choose to create an account
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Payment information

2. How We Use Your Data

Your preferences are used solely to:

  • Generate personalized movie recommendations
  • Remember your streaming platforms and languages
  • Improve the recommendation algorithm

3. Data Storage

  • Local storage: Preferences are stored on your device
  • Cloud storage: If you create an account, preferences sync via Supabase (our database provider)

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • TMDB — Movie information and posters (The Movie Database)

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use third-party analytics or tracking

5. Data Retention

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Clearing app data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

6. Children's Privacy

GoodWatch does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions? Contact: hello@goodwatch.movie
